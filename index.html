A Prayer for Peace

Lead me from death to life, from falsehood to truth. Lead me from despair to hope, from fear to trust. Lead me from hate to love, from war to peace. Let peace fill our heart, our world, our universe. Let us dream together, pray together, work together, To build one world of peace and justice for all. Peace, peace, peace.
- Adapted from the Peace Prayer of St. Francis

A World Without War

In the quiet moments between the echoes of conflict, we remember what truly matters. We remember that beneath our different languages, cultures, and beliefs, we share the same human heart that beats with hope, fears loss, and yearns for peace.

"If we have no peace, it is because we have forgotten that we belong to each other." - Mother Teresa

Let us work together to build bridges where there are divides, to extend hands where there are weapons, and to sow seeds of understanding where there is hatred. Peace is not merely the absence of war, but the presence of justice, compassion, and mutual respect.

May our collective prayers and actions create ripples that transform our world, until the day when the ruins of war become monuments to our shared commitment to peace.
